How to Play!

This is a maze game!

Tilt the maze to guide the player to the finish line!

Here are the controls:

Q is to zoom out

E is to zoom in

W is to tilt upwards

S is to tilt downwards

A is to tilt leftwards

D is to tilt rightwards
